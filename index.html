About Vine In Line

Vine In Line is a welcoming line dance community based in North San Luis Obispo County. We focus on fun, movement, and learning in a relaxed and friendly environment.

About the Instructors

Tina Scarscella

Dancing has been a hobby of Tina's since she was a teenager. It started with jazz, tap, and ballet, then to ballroom, and finally CW two step and line dancing. You could say it is in her blood!

Wendy Gray

Wendy studied jazz, tap, and ballet as a teen. In college she studied music. As an adult, she shared her love of dance by teaching adult tap classes and discovered line dancing in 2023—she’s been hooked ever since!
