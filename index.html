Welcome to Vine In Line

Vine In Line Dance teaches line dancing at all levels. However, it is a more than a line dance class -- it's a community of people who enjoy music, fun, movement, and learning in a relaxed and friendly environment.
We are based in North San Luis Obispo County. Everyone is welcome!

About the Instructors

Tina Scarsella

Dancing has been a hobby of Tina's since she was a teenager. It started with jazz, tap, and ballet, then to ballroom, and finally CW two step and line dancing. You could say it is in her blood!
📘 Facebook: Line Dancing with Tina Scarsella

Wendy Gray

Wendy studied jazz, tap, and ballet as a teen. In college she studied music. As an adult, she shared her love of dance by teaching adult tap classes and discovered line dancing in 2022—she’s been hooked ever since!
