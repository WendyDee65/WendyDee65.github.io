About Vine In Line

Vine In Line is a welcoming line dance community based in SLO North County. We focus on fun, movement, and learning in a relaxed and friendly environment.

✔ Beginner-friendly instruction
✔ No partner required
✔ Clear step-by-step teaching
✔ Focus on fun and community

About the Instructors

Tina Scarscella

Dancing has been a hobby of Tina's since she was a teenager. It started with jazz, tap, and ballet, then to ballroom, and finally CW two step and line dancing. You could say it is in her blood!

Wendy Gray

Wendy danced jazz, tap, and ballet when she was in her teen's as well. As an adult she taught an adult tap class, and started teaching line dancing in 2023. She's addicted!
