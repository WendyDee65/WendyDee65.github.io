About Vine in Line

Vine in Line is a welcoming line dance community based in SLO North County. We focus on fun, movement, and learning in a relaxed and friendly environment.

💃 About the Instructor

I started Vine in Line to share the joy of line dancing with people of all ages and experience levels.

My goal is to create a space where dancers feel comfortable learning at their own pace, enjoying great music, and building confidence on the floor.

✔ Beginner-friendly instruction
✔ No partner required
✔ Clear step-by-step teaching
✔ Focus on fun and community

What We Offer

Weekly Classes

Consistent Tuesday night classes for all levels.

Social Dancing

Practice what you learn in a fun, relaxed setting.

Workshops & Events

Special events, guest instructors, and themed dance nights.

Our Community
